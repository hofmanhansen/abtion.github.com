Take our work for it

We could write page after page about how we build amazing products. But it's not what you say, it's what you do that counts, right? So don't just take our word for it - take our work. We have collected a small selection of our most recent projects. We are proud of them all. Hope you like them too.

Game

LEGO

Our latest game for LEGO is "Who Is In The Mix" - a game based on the product series Mixels. Mixels consist of numerous funny characters that can be "mixed" in all directions and you can create new characters. In "Who Is In The Mix" you have to guess which characters have been mixed.

Web app

Cocks & Cows

With multiple awards under their belts, Cocks & Cows will serve you some of the absolute best burgers around. They have several locations in Copenhagen, and when they wanted to expand their take away services, we were excited to solve this logistic challenge.

Game

Linicin®

How do you teach children what it means to have head lice when you can not see them? You create a fun and engaging game that kids can play while they are being treated for the head lice.

Enterprise web app

Jydsk Tagteknik

Jydsk tagteknik is the biggest roofing company in Denmark. They offer free roof and insulation evaluations as part of their sales operations. They came to us with a need for a mobile evaluation and sales solution in order to optimize processes and save ressources.

Web app

AON

The purpose of this web application was to optimze and create meaningful data and reports based on a lot of unstructured information. The web application gave AON a tool to optimize and gain important and valuable knowledge from data.

Mobile app

Kappture

With an ambition to reach 200 million users within five years, we built an app that can help users remember everything, anytime in pictures.

Mobile app

TREO®

Are you aware of the factors that trigger a migraine attack? If you can track your symthons and headache type, it might be easier for you to prevent an attack in the future. "Know Your Headache" is an app that in an easy and user friendly way helps you to improve your well being and avoid headaches and migranes.
