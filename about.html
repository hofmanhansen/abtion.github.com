Technical excellence

We work with clients to ensure they have the right technical infrastructure in place, which includes systems, technology and processes. To facilitate this, we aim to take leading technology practice in order to provide unique and seamless software products and systems.

How we create value

We identify and unlock new sources of revenue related to existing and future digital assets. Our business strategists collaborate with clients to identify underlying business opportunities, new potential services, create valuable data and improve current systems and processes.

At Abtion we turn ideas and designs into functional, reliable products and services.
Our cross-disciplinary team is collaborative, and we strive to push boundaries of what software can enable for their users every day.

Our services

Web applications

When we build our web applications, we aim to build seamless, responsive products with a fast performance across all types of platforms. Our expertise within the newest and best-in-class technologies allows us to build high value, customized and beautiful products.
At Abtion we have a fast-forward approach to technology, and we try to test and implement new technologies and languages if they have proven their worth and steadiness to us. Languages that we love to work with when we build web applications are typically Ruby on Rails, React, Meteor.

Mobile Apps

When we build mobile apps our primary focus is on the user experience and the consumer engagement across devices and platforms. We build mobile apps for iOS and Android. Usually we use tools like Cordova and PhoneGap in order to create web-based apps and fast-pacing prototypes.

E-Commerce

We build and implement high-end, tech heavy and cloud-based e-commerce platforms and websites. We build in Wordpress with woocommerce, backbone, React a.o.

Games

When work become play. It’s not a secret that building games is something we love at Abtion. We are proud to have build games for some of the biggest companies in Denmark, and we strive to create the best experience for the end-user.
